## Scripts & Locks (Smart Contracts on CKB)

### What I learnt
- A brief recap of scripts and their functions to cells
- A script decides who can use a cell and under what conditions the cell can be used
- Learnt about the two important types of a script (Lock Script & Type script)
- Lock script controls who can access or unlock a cell 
- Type script controls how the cell behaves and what the cell represents
- Learnt how scripts work during transactions
- Also learnt about Hash Lock, its function, and how to deposit CKB tokens in your Hash Lock
