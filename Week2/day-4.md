### What i learned
- What tokens are 
- What fungible means and what fungible tokens are 
- Just as the Ethereum fungible tokens followa the ERC20 standards, on CKB, the equivalent is called xUDT (Extensible User Defined Tokens)
- Tokens are stored inside cells 
- Unlike many blockchains CKB tokens are controlled by scripts that allows you to define:
  - Who can Mint tokens
  - Who can transfer tokens
  - Supply rules 
  - Permissions
